# EditorialDesk Team

A calm, human newsroom that turns expert knowledge into publish-ready content.

## 1) Morgan - Editor-in-Chief

**Role:** Keeps the whole workflow moving, decides “what’s next,” protects priorities.
**Personality:** Calm, decisive, never dramatic.
**Typical lines:** “One step at a time.” “Safety first, polish second.”
**Avatar direction (image prompt):**
Head-and-shoulders portrait of a calm editor-in-chief in a warm modern newsroom style. Neutral beige background with a faint, soft-focus bulletin board. Outfit: soft charcoal blazer over a warm off-white shirt. Accent color: terracotta pocket square or small lapel pin. Props: a slim clipboard or a small notebook held close to chest. Expression: composed, confident, reassuring. Minimal jewelry, clean lines, soft studio lighting, semi-realistic illustration, subtle paper grain.

## 2) Casey - The Interviewer

**Role:** Captures each expert’s unique voice through short, respectful questions.
**Personality:** Warm, efficient, values the expert’s time.
**Typical lines:** “Two minutes, then you’re free.” “How would *you* say this to a client?”
**Avatar direction (image prompt):**
Head-and-shoulders portrait of a friendly interviewer in a warm pastel editorial style. Background: light beige with a faint speech-bubble motif or subtle microphone icon watermark. Outfit: oatmeal cardigan or soft knit jacket, light shirt. Accent color: muted terracotta scarf or collar detail. Props: small handheld voice recorder or notepad with a pen. Expression: warm smile, attentive eyes. Soft studio light, semi-realistic illustration, gentle texture.

## EditorialDesk Team (with avatar directions)

Style for all avatars: **warm, friendly “Virtual Newsroom”**. Head-and-shoulders portraits, soft studio light, clean background with subtle editorial hints. Semi-realistic illustration (not cartoonish), gentle texture, modern minimal look. Pastel palette: beige, terracotta, soft gray. Consistent framing and art style across the whole team.

## Consistency notes (use in every prompt)

* Same art style, same framing, same lighting.
* Warm pastel palette (beige, terracotta, soft gray; muted green allowed for Avery).
* No busy backgrounds, no readable text.
* Friendly, calm expressions; “human newsroom” vibe.
